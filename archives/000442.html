11.15.04

Vacation snippets

Boy is it good to be on vacation in a blue state (hardly any Bush stickers to look at).

We visited Niagara-on-the-Lake today, a wonderful Victorian resort sorta town with a ton of wineries (didn't go to any) and lots of theatre (didn't see any) and lots of shops (we definitely did the shops). I bought a really cool Irish-style hat, Darlene bought a bowl and platter from Lakeshore Pottery and we started our Christmas shopping for Darlene's family.

There are a ton of squirells who are a little too used to human beings. Darlene was about to offer a black squirrel a piece of a scone when the squirrel ran up her leg and literally ripped the food from her fingers clawing into her thumb in the process. We were STUNNED to say the least. Darlene reports that it was one hell of a strong squirrel that did not want to let go of her finger.

We've also taken in some good art at the Burchfield-Penny Art Center, some good shopping at one of the greatest grocery chains we've ever been to (Wegmans, which is out of Rochester, NY) and rewatched one of the best animated films ever made, The Iron Giant.

Photos, including the black attack squirrel will be posted when we get home.
