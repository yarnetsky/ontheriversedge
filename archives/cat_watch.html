"The Love Bug"
Darlene and I have recently watched several classic Disney flicks including "The Absent Minded Professor" and "The Shaggy Dog". Good stuff actually. The Love Bug mixed a good dose of automotive philosphy with 60s humor yet it never seemed forced. Buddy Hackett was great and Dean Jones was perfect as a person who was "half a man" if he wasn't racing cars.

"Charade"
Cary Grant and Audry Hepburn star in this thriller where you are not quite sure if Grant is a good guy or murderer, yet the two get along famously. Lots of romantic wit and suspense. Great date movie. Grade A- as Hepburn's a bit too sappy at times.

New on DVD
Two movies that just came out in video/DVD that are really good. "Finding Nemo" and "Bend It Like Beckham."
